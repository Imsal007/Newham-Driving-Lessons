Our Driving Lesson Services

We offer a complete range of driving instruction services designed to suit learners at every stage of their journey

🚗 Standard Driving Lessons

Perfect for beginners and those who prefer to learn at a steady, manageable pace. Our standard lessons are typically 1-2 hours per week, allowing time between sessions to absorb what you've learned.

  • Flexible scheduling (weekday/weekend)
  • Personalized lesson plans
  • Progress tracking and feedback
  • Door-to-door pickup in Newham areas
  • Preparation for all four test centres

⚡ Intensive Driving Course

Need to pass quickly? Our intensive courses compress learning into a shorter timeframe with multiple lessons per week or full-day sessions. Ideal for those with deadlines or limited availability.

  • Accelerated learning programme
  • Multiple lessons per week or block booking
  • Faster route to test readiness
  • Cost-effective for quick learners
  • Dedicated instructor throughout

✅ Pass Plus Course

Go beyond the basic test! Pass Plus is a DVSA-approved advanced driving course that helps new drivers build confidence and potentially reduce insurance costs by up to 35%.

  • 6 modules of advanced training
  • Town driving expertise
  • All-weather driving techniques
  • Rural road mastery
  • Dual carriageway confidence
  • Night driving skills
  • Motorway introduction (if applicable)

🛣️ Motorway Training

Since June 2018, learners can take motorway lessons with an approved instructor. Build essential confidence for high-speed driving before taking your test or driving independently.

  • Safe merging and lane changes
  • Understanding motorway signage
  • Managing speed and spacing
  • Exit strategies and planning
  • Handling large vehicles and HGVs
  • Emergency procedures

🔄 Refresher Lessons

Already have your licence but feel rusty? Whether you haven't driven in years or just need to polish specific skills, our refresher lessons will restore your confidence quickly.

  • Confidence rebuilding
  • Update on Highway Code changes
  • Specific skill focus (parking, roundabouts, etc.)
  • Pre-test warm-up sessions
  • Post-test confidence building
  • Flexible duration based on needs

📋 Mock Test Preparation

Experience the real test format before the big day. Our mock tests simulate the actual practical exam at your specific test centre, helping you understand exactly what to expect.

  • Full 40-minute test simulation
  • Test centre-specific routes
  • Detailed feedback and scoring
  • Identify weak areas before test day
  • Reduce test anxiety
  • Available for all four centres
