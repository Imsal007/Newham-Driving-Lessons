Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

1. Introduction

Welcome to DriveMaster Newham ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy rights. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our driving instruction services.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register on our website
  • Express interest in obtaining information about our services
  • Participate in activities on the website
  • Otherwise contact us

The personal information we collect may include:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number, postal address)
  • Date of birth (for DVSA test booking purposes)
  • Driver's licence number and provisional licence details
  • Payment information for lesson bookings
  • Learning progress and assessment records
  • Any other information you choose to provide

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing actions, including:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Operating system and device information
  • Referring URLs and pages viewed
  • Dates and times of website visits

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and manage driving lesson services
  • To process bookings and payments
  • To communicate with you about lessons, appointments, and test bookings
  • To maintain records of your learning progress
  • To send administrative information (updates, policy changes)
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • To comply with legal obligations and DVSA requirements
  • To improve our website and services

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your personal data based on:

  • Contract: Processing necessary to fulfil our contract with you (providing driving lessons)
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with DVSA regulations and other legal requirements
  • Legitimate Interests: For business administration and service improvement
  • Consent: Where you have given explicit consent for specific purposes

5. Disclosure of Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • DVSA (Driver and Vehicle Standards Agency): For test bookings and regulatory compliance
  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (payment processors, website hosting)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Typically:

  • Active student records: Retained throughout your learning period
  • Completed student records: Retained for 3 years after course completion
  • Website analytics: Anonymized after 26 months

7. Your Data Protection Rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request limitation of how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request transfer of your data to another organization
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data

To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@drivemasternewham.co.uk or call 07983 546733.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Secure servers and encrypted connections (SSL/TLS)
  • Limited access to personal information
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Staff training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience. Please see our separate Cookie Policy for detailed information about how we use cookies and your options regarding them.

10. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites (such as DVSA, Google Maps). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

11. Children's Privacy

For students under 18, we require parental or guardian consent before collecting personal information. Parents or guardians may request access to their child's records or request deletion by contacting us.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this policy periodically.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: info@drivemasternewham.co.uk
  • Phone: 07983 546733
  • Address: Newham, London

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) if you believe we have not complied with data protection laws.
