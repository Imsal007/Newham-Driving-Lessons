Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies on our website

Last Updated: [Insert Date]

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and provide information to website owners about how visitors use their site.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Enable basic website functions like page navigation and secure access
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting anonymous information
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings for a personalized experience
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services we use (such as Google Maps for location features)

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
session_id Essential Maintains your session during website browsing Session
_ga Analytics Google Analytics - distinguishes users anonymously 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics - tracks user behaviour patterns 24 hours
_gat Analytics Google Analytics - limits data collection on high traffic sites 1 minute

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses services from third parties that may set cookies on your device:

  • Google Maps: Used to display our service area and test centre locations. Google may collect data about your interaction with the map.
  • WhatsApp: When you click WhatsApp links, WhatsApp may set cookies for their messaging service.
  • Google Analytics: Provides anonymous statistics about website usage to help us improve our site.

We do not control these third-party cookies. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View what cookies are stored on your device
  • Delete all or specific cookies
  • Block cookies from being set
  • Set preferences for specific websites

Instructions for managing cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

Opting Out of Analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on: tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website:

  • Essential cookies cannot be disabled without preventing the website from functioning
  • Disabling analytics cookies will not affect your browsing experience but limits our ability to improve the site
  • Disabling functionality cookies means your preferences won't be remembered on return visits

7. Cookie Consent

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner. By continuing to browse the site after seeing this banner, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can change your consent preferences at any time by adjusting your browser settings.

8. Updates to This Cookie Policy
