5. Student Requirements

Valid Provisional Licence

You must hold a valid UK provisional driving licence before commencing lessons. You are responsible for ensuring your licence is valid throughout your learning period.

Minimum Age

You must be at least 17 years old to take driving lessons (or 16 if receiving certain disability benefits). For students under 18, parental or guardian consent is required.

Eyesight Requirements

You must meet the DVSA eyesight requirements, including being able to read a number plate from 20 metres away (with glasses or contact lenses if needed).
